    What exactly is Stacker?

    Stacker is a flexible software platform that lets businesses create bespoke software without writing code.

    What can I build with Stacker?

    Companies use Stacker to build software to run their operational processes.

    Common examples include bespoke CRMs, customer portals, work trackers, and business process apps.

    Why use Stacker rather than off-the-shelf software?

    Stacker allows you to create software that fits the way you work, rather than the other way round

    Since your Stacker applications are all created with no-code, you can grow and change them as your processes change.

    Is there a free trial?

    Yes, there's a 30-day free trial, so you can explore Stacker's capabilities.
